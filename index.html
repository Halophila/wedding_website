The Venue

Cambridge is a small, historic town on the Choptank River, a major tributary of the Chesapeake Bay. The city dates back to English colonists in 1684, making it one of the oldest colonial cities in Maryland and predating the United States by almost 100 years. The area is a hub for crabbing, fishing, and agriculture, and is known for being the birthplace of both Harriet Tubman and Fredrick Douglas. The wedding will be held at 447 Race Street, the former site of the Phillips Hardware Company and currently the home of The Groove Threatre Company.
